NEW MEHENDI DESIGN TRENDS IN 2020

  • Event Planet
  • March 13, 2020

And when we talk of Indian weddings. How can we not talk of mehendi. Mehendi is not only a ritual in Indian weddings but it’s a jamboree.It is celebrated a day before marriage with great pomp and show.While the bride has a very elaborate pattern done on her hands and feet, the groom usually has just a token design. In fact, applying mehndi designs is a custom during any celebration in India, be it Karva Chauth, Teej, Bhai Dooj ,Raksha Bandhan,Diwali, Ramadan or any other festival. At weddings,mehendi is a way of wishing the bride good health and prosperity as she makes her journey on to marriage.
